About me

I am a data scientist in the network research unit of Eurocontrol, where my main duties consist of researching the application of artificial intelligence models and algorithms to improve the performance of the current air traffic management system. I am an aeronautical engineer from the Technical University of Catalonia (UPC), and I hold a master in computational and applied mathematics from the Autonomous University of Barcelona (UAB). In 2017 I obtained the SESAR young Scientist Award, which aims to recognise young scientists with high potential contributing to the scientific research in the field of air traffic management and aviation. Finally, In 2019 I obtained the PhD in aerospace science and technology from the UPC. My main research areas of interest include optimal control and non-linear optimisation, Bayesian estimation as well as machine learning (in particular reinforcement learning).
